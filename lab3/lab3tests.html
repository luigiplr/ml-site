  • Study Tips

    For success, remember "SQ3R:" Survey, Question, Read, Rehearse, and Review.

    Some other good study strategies include: Study repeatedly; make the material meaningful; activate retrieval ques; use mnemonic devices; minimize interference; sleep more; and test your knowledge.

  • Don't Forget to Exercise!

    With all that studying, it can be easy to forget to exercise. Make sure to do something - run, play basketball, even just go for a walk - at least once a day. Not only will you feel better, but it will help some of what you have been studying to settle into your brain!
