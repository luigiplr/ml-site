🐾 My Dog 🐾

Kootenay

This is Kootenay. She is a 7 year old golden retriever. People in general tend to think that these dogs are friendly, intelligent, sociable... they have never met my dog. I mean, she does love people, but they don't usually love her back. Perhaps trusting a squeaky-voiced twelve-year-old with training wasn't the best idea; for all the effort I put in, she still makes Marley look like an angel.



In the summer, I take Kootenay - and a pair of speakers - down to the boat launch and throw sticks off for her. (as seen in the first picture) Here is a snippet of one of the songs I would normally listen to. It just sort of fits the atmosphere.
