Landmarks near UVic




Very Important Places: MacLauren and Bob Wright buildings for some classes, and of course Cadboro Commons building for food.


Points of Interest

Ah yes. Some very interesting places indeed. Olive Olio's is a pretty cute little place with awesome entrees for when you get sick of caff food. Then, of course, there is the dog park. Don't even get me started on the dog park. If you ever miss your dog, GO THERE. If you ever feel sad, GO THERE. It's infinitely cheaper than therapy. Then go get coffee from Olive Olio's and your life will be complete.
