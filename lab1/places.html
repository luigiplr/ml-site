Best Places To Go

With a history such as Prague's, you can't help but have cool places. Eiffel Tower – who cares? Big Ben – big deal! Come to Prague and see the beautiful cathedrals that took over six centuries to build, or an ancient clock tower with a shady past. Prague is home to so many historic and wonderful sites.

St. Vitus Cathedral

The so-called spiritual symbol of the Czech Republic! This gothic icon dominates the skyline, with due authority: it took over six hundred years to complete, and is the final resting place of kings and queens.

Astronomical Clock

This masterpiece is a must-see! Its intricacy is so magnificant that, legend says, its maker was promply blinded after its construction so that no other city could hold such wonder.

National Museum

The history of the Czech republic in artifact, scultpure, and more - this the oldest museum in the Czech Republic. It once housed the Czechoslovakian parliament, and is situated at the head of Wenceslas Square.
