Psychology 100B



In Psychology 100B, we learn the foundations of psychology. Our course covers development throughout the life-span, theories of intelligence, motivation, emotions, personality, psychological disorders and therapy, and social psychology. All these things help us to understand human behaviours and are useful in many careers we may be interested in. It also makes for a facinating class!



Faculty of Scocial Science



With seven different disciplines offered, (Anthropology, Economics, Environmental Studies, Geology, Political Science, Psychology, and Sociology) UVic Social Sciences has something for anyone curious about human interactions and society. The faculty offers lots of neat research opportunities as well, whether you want to participate as a subject or lead one yourself.



Department of Psychology

Here, we offer courses on that delve into the depths of the human mind, well as programs for both Bachelor of Science and Arts degrees.
