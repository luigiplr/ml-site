About Carnevale

When

In recent years, the Carnival management has staged a preview weekend before the traditional 11-day Carnival period. But the core carnival events (including the "Flight of the Angel" on the first Saturday of Carnevale) always take place during the main part of the festival, which follows the Roman Catholic Church calendar.

Where

The company started with organising weddings and events, gradually building up experience towards larger more complicated events and eventually expanding into Carnival Events.Carnival always ends on Martedì Grasso, or "Fat Tuesday," and Venice's celebration normally starts a week and a half earlier on a Saturday.

Origins

The day after the first procession marks the official opening of the Carnival of Venice, with another masked crowd venturing from Piazza San Marco in the afternoon and marching through the streets. Following that there are jousts and mock-military tournaments, as well as the Carnival of Venice’s most famous event, the Gran Ballo delle Maschere, a traditional costume ball that takes place at a different location each year.
